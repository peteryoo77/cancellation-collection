Meet your Care Team

Sofia Skinner, LMSW

Sofia is a dually licensed master social worker in New Jersey and New York. She has years of experience supporting survivors of acute, chronic, and complex trauma. She integrates a trauma-informed, strengths-based approach, using modalities such as motivational interviewing, CBT, CPT for PTSD, and narrative therapy. Sofia is passionate about meeting people where they are—fostering a supportive environment where clients can discover resilience and find sustainable pathways to healing. Outside of work, she enjoys quality time with her miniature schnauzer mixes Ash and Nester and following the New York Jets.

Richard Callado, PMHNP

Richard is a board-certified Psychiatric Nurse Practitioner with over 20 years of experience in mental health. He currently specializes in interventional psychiatry and treatment-resistant conditions at Curated Mental Health in NYC. His experience spans pediatric and adult psychiatry across multiple settings. He has expertise in complex psychiatric conditions, including treatment-resistant depression, schizophrenia, rapid cycling bipolar disorder, and anxiety disorders.
